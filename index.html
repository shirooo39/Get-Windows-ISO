READ ME


This site (or page) is far from done. And since it's only me working on this page, it'll take me a long and a lot of time to fully finish this page. Updating the page on slow phase.

The reason why I started doing this page was at first, for personal backup / archive. But then I decided to share it to the public and hopefully this page could help those who are looking for what they wanted.

If you're worried about downlaoding files from this page (which you should), I assure you that almost all of the ISOs came straight from Microsoft and they are not previously tempered. You can also compare the hashes to make sure if an ISO has previously been tempered or not.

This page has zero analytics whatsoever, no ads, no nothing. But it's much better if you always have your ad blocker toggled on. To avoid my files getting marked as abusive by Google (files are hosted on Google Drive), I archived the ISOs (along with their checksums (MD5, SHA-1, and SHA-256)) with compression level set to None / just archive, and named the archive in a base64 encoded name.

Since there is not much in this page, use the navigation on the left side to navigate around.

There is NO license key nor even an activator in this page!
If you need a license to activate your copy of Windows, go grab one from Microsoft themself or an official / trusted resellers.
